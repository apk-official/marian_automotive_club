About us

Who we are?

The Marian automotive is a club of students from the Marian Engineering College who design & fabricate Go - Karts which are operated by both Electric Motors and with Internal Combustion Engines.

The purpose of the club is to build up interest towards theoretical as well as practical knowledge, along with management and team work among under graduate students. It gives students hands on experience in designing & manufacturing.

About Cavallo

Team Cavallo

'Cavallo' is the name of our team run by a talented group of engineering students from Marian Engineering College,Thiruvananthapuram.

Established in the year 2019, we have got a team of technical engineers and skilled students who work very hard to come up with a high standard kart.We aim to build an academy by competing in different kart races and design competitions all over India to come out with an academy within 2-3 years of time.

Our team stands ready to help and consider every studentspersonally thus we ensure that the they get the best training and tolet the team grow in the future.

History

The path we came

The Team Cavallo is the first Go-kart team from Marian College of Engineering, a group of 15 members of 2019-2020 academic year batches. It was the journey of 7 months. The initial idea of the project was from the second year students, it was Maneesh Sir (the faculty in charge of club) the project guide who motivated and supported the students to start such a project. From the team formation in Aug, basic workshops in Sept, previrtuals in Nov and manufacturing in Dec & Jan, the combined effort of the team and the support from faculties and parents made it possible to successfully participate in two national level competitions.
